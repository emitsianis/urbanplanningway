Welcome to Urban Planning Way's official website! As you might have noticed, cities and buildings are becoming uglier and more energy consuming by the minute. But that is about to change. With our highly proffessional team of proffessionals you can forget about cities that are harder to climb than mt. Everest and 1000 euro electricity bills.
But the real question is: What can we do for YOU? Let's explore!
Urban Planning Way inc. was established in 2018 by our ambitious and insightful leader Kelly Way. Like all of the world's greatest companies and inventions, it all started with a simple idea:

What if university assignements are not actually enough to get you a job?

Dissapointed from the lack of interest in the market, Kelly decided to invest in that idea and start her own urban planning company. Little did she know at that time, that this would be a decision to be written down in history.
This is the part where any normal company would display a list of services, and give you reasons as to why you should preffer that company over their competition. We are not going to do that.

Meeting with any member of Urban Planning Way, you will be quick to realise that we don't actually know what we can do and what services we offer. To us, our work is an idea, it holds a higher meaning than concrete facts or concrete types that should be used on buildings.
Don't hesitate to contact us anytime if you have questions or you need something done. We value our relationship with people higher than anything, including urban planning itself (obviously). Also, in the off chance that you would like to work with us, send us your CV to hr@urbanplanningway.io, so that we can schedule an interview. Be prepared though, if you can describe your skills in a structured fashion and you are able to be convincing, it is highly unlikely that you will get hired.
